## Mental Wellness

**Mindset Foundation**
- Why mindset is a performance variable, not just a "nice to have"
- The identity-based habit model (2–3 sentences): focus on who you're becoming, not just what you're doing
- One reframe for the most common mental block associated with this goal

**Stress Management Techniques** (pick 3–4 most relevant to the goal)
For each, include: what it is, how to do it, and when to use it:
- Box breathing / 4-7-8 breathing
- Progressive muscle relaxation
- Journaling prompts
- Nature exposure (evidence-based dose)
- Cold exposure
- Social connection

**Mindfulness & Meditation**
- Why it supports this specific goal
- Beginner practice: 5-minute guided focus exercise (describe it in plain language)
- How to build up from 5 → 20 minutes over 4 weeks
- Recommended free apps or resources (3 max)

**Habit Building**
- The habit loop (cue → routine → reward) applied to this goal with a concrete example
- Habit stacking: 2 examples relevant to the goal
- Minimum viable habit: the smallest possible version of the target behavior
- How to handle missed days without spiraling

**Motivation & Accountability**
- Intrinsic vs. extrinsic motivation — which sustains longer and how to tap into it
- 3 accountability strategies (ranging from solo to social)
- How to measure non-scale / non-numeric progress

**When to Seek Professional Support**
List 5 signs that suggest speaking with a therapist, counselor, or mental health professional would be beneficial — framed compassionately and without stigma.
