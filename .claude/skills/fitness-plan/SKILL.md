## Fitness Plan

**Weekly Structure Overview**

Present a 7-day schedule showing workout type per day (cardio / strength / flexibility / rest / active recovery). Justify the structure based on the goal.

**Cardio Sessions** (2–3x per week)
- Type: (e.g., running, cycling, swimming, HIIT)
- Duration and intensity (moderate = can hold a conversation; vigorous = cannot)
- One beginner modification and one progression option

**Strength Sessions** (2–3x per week)

Provide a full-body or split routine appropriate to the goal. For each exercise include:
- Sets × Reps
- Target muscle group
- Beginner modification
- Form cue (one sentence)

Cover at minimum: a push, a pull, a squat/hinge, and a core exercise.

**Flexibility & Mobility** (daily or 2–3x per week)
- 5–7 stretches or mobility drills
- Hold duration / reps
- Best time to do them (morning, post-workout, evening)

**Rest & Active Recovery**
- How many full rest days per week and why
- What active recovery looks like (walk, yoga, light swim)

**Progression Guidelines**
- When to increase weight or duration (general rule: 2-rep rule, 10% weekly volume increase, etc.)
- How to know if you're overtraining (signs and what to do)

**Equipment**
- Zero-equipment version of the plan
- Minimal home equipment version ($50–$100 budget)
- Gym version
