## Nutrition Guide

**Daily Targets**

Provide general ranges (not personalized medical advice) appropriate to the goal:

| Nutrient | General Target | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Calories | Range based on goal | Deficit / maintenance / surplus |
| Protein | g per lb of bodyweight | Key for muscle / satiety |
| Carbohydrates | % of total calories | Timing matters |
| Fats | % of total calories | Focus on sources |
| Fiber | g/day | Often under-consumed |
| Water | oz/day | Adjust for activity |

**Meal Timing**
- Number of meals/snacks per day and why
- Pre-workout nutrition (what and when)
- Post-workout nutrition (what and when)
- Whether intermittent fasting is relevant to this goal, and if so, which window

**Foods to Prioritize** (list 10–12 with one-line reason each)

**Foods to Limit** (list 6–8 with one-line reason each — not "never eat" but "be mindful of")

**Simple Meal Ideas**
Provide 3 meals and 2 snacks that fit the goal. Each should include ingredients and approximate prep time. No recipes required — just the concept.

**Supplement Considerations**
List 4–5 supplements with evidence behind them for this goal. For each: what it does, typical dose, and whether it's worth considering (strong evidence / moderate / weak).

Note: Always consult a registered dietitian or physician before making major dietary changes.
