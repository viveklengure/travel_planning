## Practical Tips

**Visa & Entry Requirements (for US Citizens)**
- Current visa policy: visa-free, visa on arrival, e-visa, or embassy visa required
- Step-by-step process for whichever applies
- Cost and processing time
- Passport validity requirement (minimum months required)
- Any dual-citizenship considerations

**Currency & Money**
- Local currency name and approximate USD exchange rate
- Best ways to get local currency (ATMs, exchange bureaus, airport vs. city)
- Whether credit cards are widely accepted or cash is king
- Tipping norms by context (restaurants, taxis, hotels, tour guides)
- Any currency scams or common money traps to avoid
- Whether US dollars are accepted anywhere directly

**Getting Around Locally**
- Overview of the public transit system (metro, bus, tuk-tuk, ferry, etc.) — reliability and coverage
- Rideshare apps that work here (Uber, Grab, Bolt, local equivalents)
- Car rental: worth it or not? — road conditions, traffic, parking, international driver's license needed?
- Taxi tips: metered vs. negotiated, reputable companies, apps to use
- Intercity transport options for day trips or multi-city trips

**Connectivity**
- Best SIM card options for tourists (carriers, where to buy, cost for data)
- eSIM availability
- WiFi reliability in hotels, cafes, and public spaces
- Whether a VPN is recommended or restricted

**Health & Safety**
- Tap water safety — drink or avoid?
- Recommended vaccinations or health precautions (consult CDC/travel clinic)
- Common health risks specific to the destination (altitude, heat, mosquito-borne illness, etc.)
- Quality and accessibility of medical care for tourists
- Travel insurance: strongly recommended, optional, or essential?
- General safety level — areas to avoid, common scams targeting tourists, petty crime risks
- Emergency numbers (police, ambulance, tourist police if available)

**Cultural Etiquette**
- Dress code expectations (religious sites, conservative areas, beaches)
- Behavior norms in public spaces
- Photography etiquette (where it's prohibited or requires permission)
- Common ways tourists accidentally cause offense — and how to avoid them
- Gestures or phrases that mean something different locally

**Language**
- Official language(s)
- How widely English is spoken (tourist areas vs. off the beaten path)
- 10 useful local phrases with pronunciation guide
- Translation app recommendations for this language pair

**Packing Essentials**
- Climate-specific clothing and gear
- Items specific to activities available at this destination
- Any items that are hard to find or expensive locally
- Items that are restricted or should not be brought (e.g. medications, drone regulations)
- Power adapter type and voltage
