Generate a detailed trip overview for: $ARGUMENTS

## Trip Overview

Cover the following with specific, researched detail — avoid generic advice:

**Destination Snapshot**
- Country, region, and city/area context
- What kind of traveler this destination suits best
- One-sentence "vibe" summary (e.g. "laid-back beach town meets colonial history")

**Recommended Trip Duration**
- Minimum days to see highlights
- Ideal trip length for a well-paced experience
- Maximum before the destination feels exhausted

**Best Time to Visit**
- Month-by-month breakdown of weather (temperature ranges, rainfall, humidity)
- Peak tourist season (pros: lively; cons: crowds and prices)
- Shoulder season (best balance of weather and value)
- Off-season (what's still possible, what's closed or risky)
- Specific festivals, events, or natural phenomena tied to certain months (with dates if recurring)

**Trip Style Options**
- List at least 4 distinct trip styles this destination supports (e.g. adventure, culture, romance, family, food, wellness)
- For each style, note what makes this destination a good fit and any must-dos specific to that style

**Destination Highlights at a Glance**
- 5 things this destination is famous for
- 1–2 things that surprise first-time visitors
