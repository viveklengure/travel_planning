## Top Experiences

**Must-Do Activities** (minimum 8)
For each activity:
- Name of the experience
- Why it's unmissable — what makes it special or unique to this destination
- Practical detail: location, cost range, how long to spend, best time of day
- Insider tip to get more out of it

**Hidden Gems & Off the Beaten Path** (minimum 4)
For each:
- Name and specific location (neighborhood, address, or GPS landmark)
- Why most tourists miss it
- How to find or access it
- What to expect when you get there

**Unique Local Experiences Tourists Typically Miss**
- At least 3 experiences that require local knowledge (e.g. neighborhood markets, informal events, local hangouts)
- For each: what it is, how to find it, and why it's worth seeking out

**Day Trip Options**
For each day trip:
- Destination name and what it's known for
- Distance and estimated travel time from the main city
- How to get there (car, bus, tour, etc.)
- Top 3 things to do once there
- Whether an overnight stay is worth considering

List at least 3 day trips.

**Seasonal & Time-Sensitive Experiences**
- Festivals with specific dates or date ranges
- Natural events (migrations, blooms, auroras, etc.)
- Experiences that are only available in certain seasons
- Limited-access or permit-required spots and how to book them

**Active & Adventure**
- Hiking: best trails with difficulty, distance, and highlights
- Water sports or activities available
- Other outdoor pursuits specific to the geography (cycling, climbing, paragliding, etc.)
- Recommended operators or outfitters for guided activities

**Cultural & Historical Exploration**
- Top historical landmarks with context on their significance
- Best museums (what they cover, standout exhibits)
- Local traditions, ceremonies, or cultural practices visitors can respectfully observe or join
- Spiritual or religious sites and etiquette for visiting

**Nature & Wildlife**
- National parks or nature reserves (entry fees, best trails, opening hours)
- Scenic viewpoints with directions or access notes
- Wildlife encounters available and best times/locations
- Responsible wildlife tourism tips specific to this destination
