## Accommodation

**Best Neighborhoods / Areas to Stay**
For each area, provide:
- Name of the neighborhood/zone
- Character and vibe (1–2 sentences)
- Who it suits best (families, solo travelers, couples, party crowd, etc.)
- Proximity to main attractions and transit
- Any drawbacks to be aware of

List at least 4 distinct areas with these details.

**Accommodation Recommendations**

Provide 3 recommendations per budget tier (9 total):

*Budget (under $80/night)*
For each: property name, type (hostel, guesthouse, budget hotel), location, standout feature, and what to watch out for.

*Mid-Range ($80–$200/night)*
For each: property name, type, location, standout feature, and best room type to book.

*Splurge ($200+/night)*
For each: property name, type, location, standout feature, and what justifies the price premium.

**Accommodation Types Available**
- Overview of what styles are available at this destination (boutique hotels, eco-lodges, ryokans, riads, villas, homestays, etc.)
- Any accommodation style that is unique or iconic to this destination

**Booking Tips**
- Best platforms for this destination (Booking.com, Airbnb, local sites, etc.)
- How far in advance to book by season
- Whether negotiating directly with properties is common or effective
- Any common scams or misleading listings to watch for
