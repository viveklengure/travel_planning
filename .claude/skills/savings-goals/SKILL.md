## Savings Goals

**Emergency Fund First**
- Why it comes before investing (explain the logic)
- How to size it: 3 months (stable income/job) vs. 6 months (variable income, dependents) vs. 9–12 months (self-employed, single income household)
- Where to keep it: HYSA characteristics, what APY to look for, why not to invest it
- How to build it without disrupting other goals (split contributions)

**Goal Framework**

Help the user categorize savings goals by time horizon:

| Horizon | Timeline | Examples | Best Account Type |
|---|---|---|---|
| Short-term | 0–2 years | Vacation, car, emergency top-up | HYSA, money market |
| Medium-term | 2–5 years | Down payment, wedding, career pivot fund | CDs, I-bonds, conservative brokerage |
| Long-term | 5+ years | Retirement, financial independence | 401k, IRA, brokerage |

**Goal-Setting Math**

**Account Types Explained**
- HYSA: pros, cons, what rate to expect
- CDs: when they make sense, ladder strategy basics
- I-Bonds: inflation hedge, purchase limits, liquidity constraints
- Brokerage: for goals 5+ years out, why equities beat cash at that horizon

**Automation Strategies**
- Auto-transfer on payday (before you can spend it)
- Round-up apps: how they work, worth it or not
- Direct deposit splitting at the employer level
- Savings rate targets by life stage (20s / 30s / 40s+)

**Progress Tracking**
- How to measure progress toward each goal (% funded, months remaining)
- When to adjust a goal vs. when to stay the course
- Celebrating milestones without derailing the plan
