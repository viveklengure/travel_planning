## Getting There

Cover the following with specific, actionable detail:

**By Air**
- Nearest major international airport(s) — include airport code and distance/time to city center
- Secondary regional airports worth considering
- Airlines with direct routes from major US hubs (New York, LA, Chicago, Miami, Dallas)
- Typical flight duration from each hub
- Tips for finding cheaper fares (best booking windows, flexible date advice, budget carriers)
- Layover hubs commonly used for this route

**By Land (if applicable)**
- Driving feasibility from nearby major cities (distance, road quality, border crossings if international)
- Bus or coach options (operators, journey time, comfort level)
- Train options (operators, journey time, scenic vs. fast routes)

**By Sea (if applicable)**
- Ferry or cruise port access
- Notable cruise lines that stop here

**Airport / Port to City Center**
- All transfer options: taxi, rideshare, shuttle, metro, train, bus
- Estimated cost and travel time for each option
- Tips on avoiding tourist traps at the airport (e.g., unlicensed taxis, overpriced shuttles)

**Entry Logistics**
- Passport validity requirements for US citizens
- Visa on arrival, e-visa, or visa required — with current processing steps
- Any health or vaccination requirements
- Customs rules worth knowing (cash limits, restricted items)
