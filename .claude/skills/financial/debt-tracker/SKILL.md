**Payoff Strategies**

Compare both methods honestly:

**Avalanche Method** (mathematically optimal)
- Pay minimums on all, throw extra at highest-rate debt first
- Best for: those motivated by saving money
- Weakness: can feel slow if the highest-rate debt has a large balance

**Snowball Method** (psychologically powerful)
- Pay minimums on all, throw extra at smallest balance first
- Best for: those who need early wins to stay motivated
- Weakness: pays more interest overall
